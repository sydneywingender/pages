02

Appetite

timebox

State how much time the solution is worth before discussing scope. The appetite constrains the shape of the solution and prevents the pitch from becoming an open-ended wishlist.

Small batch

Two weeks. Choose this when the idea is a narrow improvement with limited unknowns.

Big batch

Six weeks. Choose this when the idea needs a full cycle and a meaningful end-to-end result.

Not worth betting

Use this when the problem is real, but the solution is not valuable enough for the time requested.

Chosen appetite: [State the exact appetite and explain why this amount of time is appropriate.]
